Watching spring arrive from my home office.

June 2021

During the winter of 2020, I hooked up a camera to a Raspberry Pi and started writing python modules to do time lapse photography. Early on, I made some films of winter storms coming in over the course of 4-12 hour periods.

At the start of spring, I set up for a longer time lapse; I wanted to film the start of spring as seen from the window of my home office. There's a lot of greenery outside so I was hoping to quantify the proportion of the color space occupied by green over time.
