2020: The high and low temperatures

January 2021

Two years ago, I hooked up a DS18B20 thermometer to a Raspberry Pi, and I set up a cron job to save the temperature in my postgres database every minute.

A year later, in early 2020, I posted a year-in-review style article describing the data I had obtained from running the cron job for a full calendar year. Well, we have another year of data and so here I am describing the latest in temperatures from my apartment!

(FYI- You can still check out a real time view of the data via my heroku webapp.)
